    If you want peace of mind that your road cargo arrives at its destination safely and on time, talk to us. We ensure daily departures and reliable transit times across more than 35 countries in Europe – and provide road freight services in North America, South Africa and the Middle East. 

    Request a quote for shipment

    Flexible, safe and efficient

    We specialize in road transport services tailored to suit your exact needs and cargo requirements, whether you're importing or exporting. We offer part load, full load and groupage transport  that is flexible, safe and efficient. With expertise in handling both palletised and non-palletised goods, to ensure the transportation of your cargo. Additionally, we offer international intermodal solutions, seamlessly integrating air , sea  or rail transport  when needed for your cargo to reach its final destination on time.

    AMG's dedicated teams with local experts, are ready to take care of all your road transport requirements. Choose AMG for trusted and efficient transportation services.

    Experts in road freight

    As one of the leading international road freight providers, we have dedicated teams of road cargo experts ready to take care of your road transport needs with global road cargo know-how and local market understanding. 

    Wherever you’re based, you’ll find that predictable delivery times are standard for our road freight services. That’s because our teams know what it takes to avoid delays and unwanted surprises on the road and get cargo to its destination safely and efficiently.

    Our road freight services cover all types of cargo including:

    • Chilled and ambient temperature goods

    • High-value goods

    • Sensitive and perishable cargo

    • Fragile goods

    • Dangerous goods

    • Time-critical deliveries

    Benefit from our customs expertise

    If you work with international road freight, you need to stay up to date with all relevant road freight transport agreements and legislation. We ’re familiar with the documentation needed to keep your delivery on the road so you can have peace of mind that your cargo is on its way to its destination and not held up waiting on paperwork.
