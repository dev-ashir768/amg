    Based on 5 criteria

    In order to ensure that we can offer what we believe are the best possible industry solutions
    we have defined five criteria for our connectivity solutions:

    Accurate

    Accuracy is essential in our fast moving and integrated business, our customers need to know where their shipments are and when they will be arriving.

    Up-to-the-minute

    Accuracy by itself isn't always enough as most of our customers also need up-to-the-minute reporting on the status of their shipments, in order for them to be able to efficiently plan their business, or to
    communicate to their customers.

    Secure

    AMG is renowned throughout the industry for data security and integrity. Our customers can relax in the knowledge that their data is both secure and complete.

    Easy manageable

    IT systems need to be installed, they need to be efficiently operated and the data easily filed and obtained. Our data connectivity systems are quickly installed, and are either free or offer generous installation rates and are designed to run on existing client platforms.

    OR

    Greater flexibility

    With increases in data and data overview we are seeing new opportunities to provide a degree of flexibility, offering our customers potential for savings or providing new services to their clients.

    Naturally we have developed these criteria in order to give all of our customers added value across the
    range of their operations, with the potential for measurable cost savings.

    The five criteria are woven into our three solutions that are designed to meet the needs of our customers
    no matter the size or nature of their business.

    How you choose the right solution

    The solution that is right for you starts with a dialogue. Through the on-boarding process with AMG we will together find the right solution that fits your specific need and plan for getting it set up.

    All EDI integrations come with a cost. EDI Plus comes with a significantly higher price, as it is a customized EDI integration.
    Public API and myAMG are offered to all customers free of charge.
    Contact us to discuss the best solution for your business, whether it is our standard EDI, EDI Plus, or API.
    * The customer will be charged when using eDC
