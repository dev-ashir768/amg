    AMG General Privacy Information Notice

    AMG is committed to protecting your personal data and ensuring that organisational and technical security measures are in place to protect your personal data, whether you are buying AMG's transport and/or logistics services (“Services ”), receiving newsletters or simply browsing AMG's websites.

    By using AMG's websites or buying Services, you consent to the processing of your personal data by the AMG Group as outlined in this AMG General Privacy Information Notice (the “Policy ”) and the Legal Notice pertaining to the use of the AMG website. The Legal Notice can be found here .

    AMG is a global group of companies within the field of global transportation and logistics. In order to conduct our daily business, the AMG Group entities on a regular basis exchange data, including personal data. All such transfers take place in accordance with applicable local legislation and EU ’s General Data Protection Regulation. In addition, AMG has implemented Binding Corporate Rules (“BCR ”), which have been approved by applicable public authorities. The BCR relates to the processing and transfer within the AMG Group of personal data originating from the EU/EEA. This means that all AMG Group entities will comply with the same internal set of rules for all such transfers.

    AMG ’s Binding Corporate Rules can be found here  

    The AMG entity you are in contact with is the data controller of the personal data received and processed about you as part of providing Services to you or as part of you visiting any of AMG's websites.

    AMG may change and update this Policy on a regular basis at its sole discretion.

    Personal Data, Purposes of Processing and Legal Basis

    Services

    AMG receives and collects personal data about you directly from you or from other data controllers for the purpose of AMG providing or assisting with Services or requests to or from you as the beneficiary of a Service. Other data controllers may disclose personal data to us such as your name, address, title or contact information, as necessary for us to provide Services to you. Upon your request, AMG will provide you with the identity of such other data controllers.

    Your personal data is processed to fulfil the agreement that you have entered into with AMG or AMG's customer or in order to take steps to provide or assist with your requests (e.g. while completing a contact form, as part of a registration or a survey).

    AMG processes your personal data in accordance with applicable laws and the legal basis for the processing of your personal data is either a contract, your consent, legal obligation or AMG's legitimate interest in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation article 6, §1, point a, b, c and f.

    Websites

    When you use AMG ’s websites, information and data from your computer system  may be collected and logged by us as is necessary for the functionality of AMG ’s websites, and in accordance with AMG ’s Cookie Policy, found on www.dsv.com . This may include processing of personal data, e.g. IP address, access time and other website and browser information. Further, AMG may collect your name and contact information if you use a request form on any AMG website.

    AMG processes your personal data in accordance with applicable laws, and the legal basis for the processing of your personal data is either your consent or AMG ’s legitimate interest in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation article 6, § 1, point a and f.

    N ewsletters and Other Commercial Materials from AMG

    AMG processes your name, e-mail and other contact information in order to send you newsletters, invitations and information about events and other relevant information about AMG's Services and the company.

    AMG processes your personal data in accordance with applicable laws and the legal basis for the processing of your personal data is your consent in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation article 6, §1, point a. You can always withdraw your consent for the continued processing.

    Regardless of your purpose, it is recommended that you do not disclose to us any sensitive personal data such as racial or ethnic origin, religion, trade union membership, sexual orientation, health, genetic or biometric data etc.

    Further, AMG may anonymise and aggregate data, including personal data, gathered via any AMG website or through any booking system or similar for statistical purposes to expand AMG's product portfolio and improve the Services.

     

    Disclosure of Personal Data to Third Parties

    AMG may disclose your personal data to other entities in the AMG Group, contracted service providers and advisors (“Third Parties ”), if it is necessary in order to fulfil its obligations towards you. Contracted service providers can for example be hauliers, shipping lines, airlines, public authorities or IT-providers, providing IT-tools related to part of the Services such as track &trace, labelling and booking. 
     
    Further, AMG may disclose personal data processed in connection with the use of AMG's websites, your participation in events or receiving of commercial information from AMG to hosting companies, marketing companies or other providers necessary to provide the Services. 
     
    Further, AMG may disclose your information in order for AMG to comply with its legal obligations, to protect the rights, property and safety of AMG or others or in case of a sale of/liquidation of any part of AMG's business or assets.
     
    Upon your request, AMG can provide you with the identity of such third parties. 
     
    The third parties to whom the personal data is disclosed may be located outside your country of domicile, but AMG will always ensure an adequate level of personal data protection and ensure that a lawful method for the transfer and disclosure of personal data is introduced, e.g. EU Standard Contract Clauses.
     

    The Period AMG Process Your Personal Data

    Your personal data is processed for as long as it is necessary in order to fulfil the purpose and in accordance with applicable law. Your personal data will be deleted when it is no longer necessary to keep in order to fulfil the purpose, unless applicable laws require AMG to keep it for a longer period. For how long the purpose exists  vary depending on the legal basis and circumstances around it (e.g. terms agreed in a contract with you, validity period of consent, legal interest, etc.), countries to which the Services relate (e.g. national bookkeeping requirements etc.) and what services AMG provides.

    Personal data related to the use of AMG's websites will be deleted after 30 days at the latest, except from cookies, which will be deleted in accordance with AMG's Cookie Policy.

    If the processing of your personal data is based on your consent, the personal data will be deleted when you withdraw your consent at the latest, unless applicable law require AMG to store the personal data for a longer period.

    Your Rights as an Individual

    In accordance with laws, you have the following rights:

    • You have the right to be informed about the personal data AMG are processing about you.
    • You have the right to have incorrect or incomplete personal data corrected or completed.
    • You have under certain circumstance right to have your personal data deleted.
    • You have under certain circumstances the right to restrict and limit the processing of your personal data.
    • You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data.
    • You have under certain circumstances the right to request that your personal data is transmitted to another data controller.

    Any consent that you may have provided to AMG may be withdrawn at any time by contacting us using the contact details provided below.

    Data subject requests, Data breach, Contact

    If you have any questions regarding the content of this policy or your rights as an individual, you can choose to make use of our DSAR webform to exercise your rights or with any concerns in this regard and we will try to get back to you in a timely manner.

    If you have noticed a breach of personal data where AMG is involved, please report it using our data breach webform . We will then make sure to act on it accordingly as quickly as possible.
